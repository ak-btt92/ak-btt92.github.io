Hanamigawa, Chiba, Japan

Hanamigawa, my hometown is located in Chiba City, Chiba Prefecture, Japan. Chiba City is the capital and largest city of Chiba Prefecture, which is a part of the Kanto region of Honshu. The area is located on the eastern side of Tokyo Bay, and thirty-seven kilometers eastward away from the centre of Tokyo. You can access here within one hour from Tokyo station.

Danchi

Hanamigawa Danchi:団地 is bedroom suburb of the Greater Tokyo Area with a massive group of reasonable apartment buildings developed during the period of rapid economic growth in Japan after the Second World War. Even though it's been aging year by year, yet the town is beautifully maintained with lush green parks and modernised public facilities. Nearby the town there are a rich variety of restaurants and some great shopping parks, and even the Tokyo Disney Resort. People are also able to access a number of exciting cities in Tokyo Metropolis within sixty to ninety minutes by car or train.
